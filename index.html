Our Solution

ChirpOut is a secure, anonymous platform for professionals to safely report concerns they have with their workplace. Its automated data analysis processes then aggregate reports to discover new or ongoing workplace trends. All analyses are published, for anyone to view at anytime.

Anonymous Reporting

Chirp your workplace concerns with our completely anonymous reporting tool.

Report Analysis

Have your concern amplified by contributing as a key data point to your workplace's environment report.

Workplace Dashboards

Freely browse trends across your workplace, industry, or region with our suite of data analysis tools, viewable on a customizable dashboard.

Security Assurance

Your anoniminity and safety is our first priority.
